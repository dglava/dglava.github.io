16 Horsepower - Scrawled in Sap

The sheet-music is for the album recording, with the slide guitar in open G minor tuning. Since I don't have a guitar constantly set-up for that tuning, I've adapted it for a standard tuned guitar without a slide. It sounds great if you play 2nd inversions on the 2nd, 3rd and 4th strings. Play them with slight overdrive, near the bridge, sliding between the triads.
