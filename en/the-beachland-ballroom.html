Idles - The Beachland Ballroom

I know exactly what kind of feelings this song evokes, but I can't describe it. Persistent, thumping, determined, powerfull and desperate.

The first part is in E major, the second one in D major. The tempo is around 90 bpm. It's an incredibly simple song to play. The first part only has you playing a F# power chord; the second part a D-B-F#-A powerchord progression. It doesn't take away from the impact the song has.

I usually play along to this with a backing track. I can upload it if there's interest.
