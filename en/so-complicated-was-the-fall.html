Clann Zu - So Complicated Was the Fall

It's such a shame that this group disbanded.

I think they're using a violin run through a distortion pedal for the intro. The tone is really thick and I'm not sure if there's also a 2nd violin or guitar playing. That's especially true for the part which starts at bar 113. I spent a lot of time trying to get the drums right. After all, they contribute so much to this track. Only once I started to transcribe them have I noticed how cool it was, with its snare rolls and almost jazzy feel. The part starting at bar 73 was pretty hard to transcribe as a non-drummer, so there might be some mistakes.

I used one of those AI stem splitting tools for the first time while transcribing this. While the quality is lacking for using it in my backing tracks, it's more than sufficient for transcribing duties.
