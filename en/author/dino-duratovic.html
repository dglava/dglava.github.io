Tabs/sheet-music for Sébastien Tellier - Roche.

Sometimes I wonder how this one would have turned out had it been on his first album instead of Sexuality. There's a certain melancholic tone to this, which is just enough to prevent it from being cheesy trash. I adore it.

The same chord progression goes on throughout the song. This is how I play it on the guitar. The original is in F minor, but I'm playing it in D minor due to technical reasons (slightly broken guitar).
