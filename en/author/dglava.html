Tabs for Aereogramme - Descending.

At first I disliked the noise on the left channel during the first part of the song. It doesn't grow on you over time, but you learn to appreciate its roughness in terms of the complete picture. Everything is slowly coming into focus and when it finally does, it's lost again.

Drop the 6th string down to D. I don't play it exactly as it's shown here.

The intro is basically the same as the 2nd vocal part, just without singing. For the 1st vocal part, listen to the song to get the picking pattern. In the 2nd vocal part, add emphasis to the C-B, B-C parts and the E-G, G-E parts.
