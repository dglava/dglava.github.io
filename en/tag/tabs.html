So Complicated Was the Fall

Published: Fri, 01 Mar 2024

In: Stvari

Tags: tabs sheet-music

Translations: bs

Clann Zu - So Complicated Was the Fall

It's such a shame that this group disbanded. I don't know if they're aware how special these tracks are. So Complicated Was the Fall is one of them. I think they're using a violin run through a distortion pedal for the intro. The tone is really thick and I'm not sure if there's also a 2nd violin playing. That's especially true for the part which starts at bar 113.

I used one of those AI stem splitting tools for the first time while transcribing this. While the quality is lacking for using it in my backing tracks, it's more than sufficient for transcribing duties.
